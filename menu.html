Burger King Breakfast Menu

Served daily from 6:00 AM until 10:30 AM (weekdays) / 11:00 AM (weekends)

9 items

The Burger King breakfast menu is built around the iconic Croissan'wich range — warm, flaky croissant buns filled with fresh egg, melted cheese, and a choice of flame-grilled or crispy proteins. Whether you're a fan of beef, chicken, or the indulgent combination of sausage and bacon, there's a Croissan'wich crafted for your morning preference. Alongside the sandwiches, the Burger King breakfast menu features the hearty Breakfast King for those who want a full burger-style experience in the morning, crispy Potato Bites as the perfect side, and a comprehensive hot drinks range including espresso, cappuccino, latte, flat white, hot chocolate, and tea. All breakfast items are prepared fresh and served hot — the ideal fuel for whatever the day ahead holds.

Breakfast at Burger King sets itself apart from the competition through its commitment to premium ingredients, including the signature croissant bun that elevates every sandwich above standard fast-food fare. The flame-grilling technique applied to beef proteins gives the breakfast items a genuinely distinctive smoky flavour. For the best breakfast experience, we recommend the Fully Loaded Croissan'wich with Egg paired with Potato Bites and your choice of hot drink.

Burgers & Sandwiches

Flame-grilled to perfection — the heart of the Burger King menu

22 items

The burger range is the cornerstone of the Burger King menu and the reason the brand has maintained its position as one of the UK's most beloved fast-food chains for decades. Every beef burger in this range is flame-grilled — not fried, not microwaved, but cooked over real flames in a method that creates genuine char marks and a smoky depth of flavour that customers recognise and crave. The iconic WHOPPER® leads the range, a quarter-pound+ beef patty loaded with fresh vegetables and mayo on a toasted sesame bun. Alongside the WHOPPER family, you'll find the Big King range (BK's multi-patty option), the Chicken Royale family (the UK's favourite crispy chicken burger from BK), and premium options including the Wagyu Wellington and plant-based alternatives for those seeking meat-free meals.

Drinks & Beverages

Refreshing, energising, and warming — the complete BK drinks menu

The Burger King drinks menu covers every need, from classic soft drinks and frozen slushies to premium energy drinks and a comprehensive hot beverages range. For breakfast, the hot drinks selection is particularly noteworthy — encompassing espresso, Americano, cappuccino, latte, flat white, hot chocolate, and tea, all made with quality coffee beans and properly prepared to coffee-shop standards. The Frozen Fanta range — available in Blue Raspberry, Strawberry, and a Strawberry & Blue Raspberry mix — has become a summer favourite, while the Monster Energy range caters to those who need a more powerful morning or afternoon energy boost. For children and those seeking non-carbonated options, the Innocent Kids Juicy Water range provides a healthy, fruit-based hydration option.
