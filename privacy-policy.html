Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for Burger King Breakfast Menu (https://burgerkingbreakfastmenu.co.uk/). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. Last Updated: 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our website.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

Contact Forms & Email Inquiries: When you contact us via email or contact forms, we collect information such as your name, email address, phone number (if provided), and the content of your message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and communicate with you.

Feedback & Suggestions: If you provide feedback, comments, or suggestions about our website or content, we may collect and store this information.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Website Usage Data: Browser type and version, operating system, pages visited and time spent on each page, links clicked, referring website addresses, and search terms used on our site.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These help us remember your preferences and understand how visitors use our site. Most browsers allow you to control cookie settings.

IP Address & Location Data: We collect your IP address, which may indicate your general geographic location (city/region level, not precise). This helps us understand traffic patterns and improve our service.

Analytics: We use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to track website performance and user behavior. This data is aggregated and anonymous and helps us improve content and functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Responding to Inquiries – To answer your questions and address your concerns
  • Website Improvement – To analyze how visitors use our site and enhance user experience
  • Content Optimization – To understand which content is most valuable to our audience
  • Technical Support – To troubleshoot technical issues and maintain website functionality
  • Analytics – To generate aggregate insights about website traffic and user behavior
  • Legal Purposes – To enforce our terms and conditions and protect our legal rights

3. Sharing & Disclosure of Information

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share non-personally identifiable information with web hosting providers, analytics services (such as Google Analytics), email service providers, and content delivery networks. We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect the safety of users, or detect fraud.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical, administrative, and physical security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Security measures include SSL/TLS encryption, secure servers and firewalls, regular security updates and monitoring, and limited access to personal information.

Important Note: No security system is impenetrable. While we implement strong security measures, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help websites remember your preferences and track your activity. We use cookies for essential functions, user preferences, analytics, and third-party services. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

6. Third-Party Links & Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are NOT responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. This Privacy Policy applies only to our website.

7. Third-Party Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect and analyze website usage data. Data collected is subject to Google's privacy policy. You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is NOT directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information should contact us at bgfastmenu@gmail.com.

9. Data Retention

  • Contact Information – Retained for up to 2 years or until you request deletion
  • Cookies – Typically expire after 1-2 years
  • Analytics Data – Aggregated and anonymized data retained indefinitely
  • Email Inquiries – Retained for reference and compliance purposes

10. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. You can opt-out of email communications, manage cookies through your browser, and use Google Analytics opt-out tools.

11. GDPR & EU Regulations

If you are a resident of the European Union, United Kingdom, or European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the GDPR including: right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and the right to object to processing.

12. CCPA & California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the CCPA. We do not sell personal information. For CCPA requests, contact us at bgfastmenu@gmail.com.

13. International Data Transfers

Our website is hosted and operated in the United Kingdom. If you access our website from outside the UK, your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the UK and other countries where our service providers operate.

14. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the website after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
